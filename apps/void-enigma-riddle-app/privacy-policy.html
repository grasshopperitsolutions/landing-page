2. What data the app collects

The App is designed as a simple riddle generator and does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The App:

  • Does not collect or store your contacts, photos, messages, precise location, or payment information.
  • Does not include in-app advertising SDKs or third-party tracking libraries in its current version.

If you later add analytics, crash reporting, or ads, you must update this policy to describe exactly what is collected and why.

3. Use of the Gemini API

When you request a new riddle, the App sends a text prompt to the Google Gemini API to generate a riddle and answer.

The content sent is limited to generic instructions (for example, asking for a short, fun riddle) and does not include personal identifiers about you. The AI response (riddle and answer) is displayed on your device and may be temporarily stored in the App’s memory to avoid showing the same riddle repeatedly in the same session.

For more information about how Google handles data, please review the Gemini API and related privacy documentation provided by Google.

4. How we use and store data

Within the App, we may temporarily store:

  • The current riddle and answer.
  • A short in-memory list of previously shown riddles in this session, to reduce repetition.

This data stays on your device’s memory while the App is running and is cleared when you reset the history or close the App. We do not create long-term user profiles from this information.

We do not use this data for marketing, profiling, or tracking across apps or devices.

5. Third-party services

Besides the Google Gemini API used to generate riddle text, the App does not integrate other third-party services such as analytics, advertising networks, or social plugins in its current version.

If additional third-party services are added in future updates, this policy will be updated to describe what data those services receive and for what purpose.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and generates riddles that are meant to be suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data security

Because the App does not create user accounts or store personal data on our own servers, security risks are limited.

Data transmitted to the Gemini API uses HTTPS, which helps protect it in transit; however, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
