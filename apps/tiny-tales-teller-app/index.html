Parental Peace of Mind

We designed this app with children's safety as the top priority. All generated stories adhere to strict content safety guidelines, ensuring the narratives are positive, educational, and free from inappropriate material.

Our Commitment to Privacy

The Tiny Tales Teller app is provided by **Grasshopper IT Solutions, LLC**. We do not require accounts, user profiles, or collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like names, emails, or precise location from children or adults.

Data Interaction (Gemini & TTS)

When a story is generated, the app sends a prompt (based on your selections: e.g., "Write a story about a brave fox and friendship") to the **Google Gemini API** for text and a **TTS API** for audio.

  • **Input Data:** Limited to generic selections (character, theme, tone) and **does not** contain personal identifiers.
  • **Generated Content:** The story text and audio are rendered on your device and are **not** stored on our servers.
  • **Child Safety:** We are committed to COPPA compliance. We do not knowingly solicit or store personal information from children under 13.
